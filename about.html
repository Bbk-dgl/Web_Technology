About Dolakha

Dolakha is a picturesque district in eastern Nepal, renowned for its historical temples, stunning mountains, trekking trails, and rich cultural heritage. It offers an incredible mix of natural beauty and spiritual significance, including the sacred Bhimeshwar Temple and Kalinchok Bhagwati Temple.

This portal is designed to guide tourists and adventurers through Dolakha's top attractions, trekking routes, cultural experiences, and travel tips. From adventure trekking to cultural tours, Dolakha provides a truly unique experience for every traveler.

Why Visit Dolakha?

Historical Temples

Visit sacred sites like Bhimeshwar Temple and Kalinchok Bhagwati, rich with centuries of history.

Trekking & Nature

Explore breathtaking trails in Kalinchok, Sailung, Tsho Lake, and Jiri, surrounded by nature.

Culture & Festivals

Experience vibrant local festivals, traditional music, cuisine, and Dolakha’s rich heritage.
